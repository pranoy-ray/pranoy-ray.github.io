Pranoy is a Postdoctoral Researcher at the [Center for Organic Battery Innovation (COBI)](https://organicbattery.climate.uchicago.edu/). His research develops data-efficient screening methods to support physical experiments in the pursuit of accelerated battery material design and discovery. He is currently advised by [Dr. Laura Gagliardi](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Laura_Gagliardi) and [Dr. Rajeev Assary](https://www.anl.gov/profile/rajeev-surendran-assary). He earned his Ph.D (Mechanical Engineering) in May 2026 from the Georgia Institute of Technology, Atlanta, advised by [Dr. Surya R. Kalidindi](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=C2k6mYwAAAAJ&hl=en). 

He is a computational materials science researcher and data scientist specializing in molecular structure screening using spatial statistics. His work develops feature engineering workflows that inform robust material process-structure-property relationships across chemical processes, integrating physics-based constraints with data-driven models on HPC systems. He has experience in driving technical deliverables for chemical, semiconductor, and federal lab partners, and led diverse research teams in fast-paced environments.
