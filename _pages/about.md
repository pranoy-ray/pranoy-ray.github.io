Pranoy is a Postdoctoral Researcher at the [Center for Organic Battery Innovation (COBI)](https://organicbattery.climate.uchicago.edu/), co-advised by [Dr. Laura Gagliardi](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Laura_Gagliardi) and [Dr. Rajeev Assary](https://www.anl.gov/profile/rajeev-surendran-assary). As a computational materials and data scientist, his current work develops data-efficient screening methods to accelerate battery material design and discovery. By integrating physics-based constraints with data-driven models on high-performance computing (HPC) systems, he creates feature engineering workflows that establish robust process-structure-property relationships and directly support physical experiments.

He earned his Ph.D. in Mechanical Engineering in May 2026 from the Georgia Institute of Technology, advised by [Dr. Surya R. Kalidindi](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=C2k6mYwAAAAJ&hl=en), where he specialized in molecular structure screening using spatial statistics. Throughout his doctoral research and past projects, he drove key technical deliverables for chemical, semiconductor, and federal lab partners, and successfully led diverse research teams in fast-paced environments.
